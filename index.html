How To Play

 

1. Make sure to allow pop ups, if prompted!

 

2. To play, just enter your channel name, then press "Start Game" button.

 

Side Notes

 

- You don't have to reset the game if you change any of the settings, except the channel name.

 

- There is no need to add a prefix like "!word" to make a guess.

 

- The word can be anywhere in a message. Unless "Detect First Words Only" is checked

 

- Words joined together must be guessed together.
